      religious practices
      grievance:委屈；抱怨；牢骚；不平的事

  • How many amendments does the Constitution have?

    • Twenty-seven

      amend:修订（法律文件、声明等）
      reinterpret:重新解释；重新诠释
      overrule:（以权力）否定;推翻
      collectively:集体；共同；统；蜂拥

  • What did the Declaration of Independence do?

    • Announced our independence from Great Britain
    • Declared our independence from Great Britain
    • Said that the United States is free (from Great Britain)

      convene:召集；集合
      to declare – to officially state that something is true
      act:行动；法令
      boycott:抵制；拒绝购买（或使用、参加）

  • What are two rights in the Declaration of Independence?

    • Life
    • Liberty
    • Pursuit of happiness

      unalienable rights:不可剥夺的；不可分割的；不可让渡的
      synonymous with:同义的；等同于…的
      rags-to-riches story
      to be exemplified by (something)

  • What is freedom of religion?

    • You can practice any religion, or not practice a religion

      separation of church and state
      endorse:赞同；认可；签署

  • What is the econnomic system in the United States?

    • Capitalist economy(资本家；资本主义者)
    • Market economy(市场经济)

  • What is the “ rule of law”?

    • Everyone must follow the law
    • Leaders must obey the law
    • Government must obey the law
    • No one is above the law

      Magna Carte:《英格兰大宪章》;宪章革命
      to put in place – to establish something
      totalitarianism:极权主义；极权政治
      to be exempt from (something):豁免
      due process:正当程序；正当法律程序

  • Name one branch or part of the government

    • Congress/legislative
    • President/executive
    • The courts/judicial

      appropriations:拨款；经费

  • What stops one branch of government from becoming too powerful?

    • Checks and balances
    • Separation of powers

      majority vote
      life term

  • Who is in charge of executive branch?

    • The President

  • Who makes federal laws?

    • Congress
    • Senate and House(of Pepresentatives)
    • (U.S. or national) legislatatives

  • What are the two parts of the U.S. Congress?

    • The Senate and House (of Representatives)

  • How many U.S. Senater are there?

    • 100

  • We elect a U.S. Senator for how many years?

    • 6

  • Who is one of your state’s U.S. Senators?

    • Answers will vary. [For District of Columbia residents and residents of U.S.
      territories, the answer is that D.C. (or the territory where the applicant lives) has no U.S. Senators.]

  • The House of Representatives has how many voting members?

    • 435

  • We elect a U.S. representative fo hwow many years?

    • 2

    session=meeting - Quiet, please! This court is now in session.
    poll - 民意调查
    pressing issue - a topic that is extremely important
    constituents - 成员；成分

    • (Because of) the state’s population

      proportional - people pay should be proportional to the amount
      of money they make
      district - Which school district is your high school in?
      gerrymandering - 选区划分不公

    • November

      voting booth - 投票间
      ballot - to cast a ballot=to vote

    • residency - where a person has lived and for how long

    administration - 行政管理部门

    • The vice president.

      swearing-in ceremony - 就职仪式
