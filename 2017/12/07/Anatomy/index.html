Legal Terminology

cornea(角膜): At the front part of the eye

achilles tendon(跟腱): At the back of the leg, connecting the calf(小腿肚) to the heel(足跟) bone

appendix(阑尾): A tube connected to a pouch called the cecum colon(盲肠)

adrenal glands(肾上腺): Above the kidneys

biceps(二头肌): Are muscles in the upper arm, between the shoulder and the elbow

triceps(三头肌): Are muscles on the back of the upper arm

quadriceps(四头肌): Are a muscles group on the front of the thighs(大腿)

hamstrings(腿后肌): Are muscles on the back of the thighs

thyroid glands(甲状腺): At the front of the neck

lungs(肺): Are high up in the chest

bladder(膀胱): Sits on the pelvic-floor(骨盆底)

heart(心脏): In the middle of the chest, slightly towards the left

stomach(胃): Towards the left of the upper abdomen

spleen(脾): In the upper left of the abdomen
