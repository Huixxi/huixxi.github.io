Dining Etiquette（西方餐饮礼仪文化）

Posted on 2017-12-29   |   In Non-Tech   |  

Ladies First(女士优先)

When you’re having lunch and dinner with a lady, she is always first.

  • When she sits…
  • When she orders…

Your Napkin(餐巾)

No napkin on table, even under the plate! ! !

Fold the napkin, and tile it on your lap.
Make sure that the open side of the napkin is facing you.— Why?
One reason: It’s good for gentleman, but also for ladies wearing the lipstick(口红), because you will use the inside of your napkin to wipe your mouth, and make sure you keep the outsides always clean. Especially when you are having a white napkin.
Notice： Only your mouth, you will never blow your nose or wipe the table with your napkin.

Three-Course Meal(三道菜)

Starter(开胃小食)

  • Mushroom and truffle soup(蘑菇松露汤)
    You can have bread with that. Better not to dip(蘸), use your spoon.

Main course(主食)

  • Beefsteak(牛排)
    Real beef, any piece like steak, tenderloin(嫩腰肉), sirloin(牛里脊肉), rib eye(牛里脊肉), tomahawk steak(战斧牛排)… Not messy, not bones, with some vegetables, easy and nice.

Dessert(甜食)

  • The chocolate dessert
    Chocolate nmusse, chocolate cake, chocolate icecream…

Wine(红酒)

  • Brodeaux or England
    Usually bear your wine while with your main course, with nice piece of beef.

Cutlery(餐具)–fork and knife

Left fork & Right knife

Only one way to hold your cutleries – With your index(食指) on top of your fork and knife and always keep the tins(叉头) down and blade(刀刃) down.
Fork is only pick the food, and knife is only to cut the food.

How to leave table property?

On the very formal dinner, you should never leave the table during the meal, never ever.
But when you have to leave the table, just leave loosely your napkin on the chair, so that others will know you’ll back.

Always be polite and pleasant – No phones on the table.

Don’t leave your smart phone on the table, keep it in your jacket. Keep eye contact.

Bread skills

How to choose your bread from this basket?
First thing: Select with your eyes, you won’t touch everything. Only to pick the one you favorite, and then you can select with your hand.
Remember: The bread is the only you can eat with your hands.

Confidently

As a starter, I’ll go for…
And as main course, I’ll go for..
When you have a data, don’t try to order anything too messy, too creamy(油腻).
Real beef with vegetables is always a manly easy meal.

Two ways to hold your wine class

Don’t try this.

  • Always by the leg(杯脚), with your thumb and two fingers.
  • Hold the plate.

Only cheer once – At the begining of the dinner.
